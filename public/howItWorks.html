PlaceFindr.com

Tutorial

Welcome to PlaceFindr.com! Our goal is to match you with the perfect place to live or travel to.

To get started, all you need to do is select a value for each slider in the preference tab. Then, when you've selected the values corresponding to your ideal living conditions, just press the search button and you will be provided with a heatmap of all GPS locations in the United States that match your preferences.

Saving Your Preferences

If you would like to save a preference, all you need to do is click the save preference button, provide a name for your preference and click confirm. All of your saved preferences will be listed on the "Saved Preferences" page and will stay there until they are manually deleted.
