Tutorial

Welcome to PlaceFindr.com!

Our goal is to match you with the perfect place to live or travel to. To get started, select a value for each slider in the preference tab. Once you've selected the values corresponding to your ideal living conditions, press the search button. You will then be provided with a heatmap of all GPS locations in the United States that match your preferences.

Saving Your Preferences

If you would like to save a preference, click the save preference button, provide a name for your preference, and click confirm. All of your saved preferences will be listed on the "Saved Preferences" page and will stay there until manually deleted.
